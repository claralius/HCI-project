HERO SELECTION

There are currently 119 heroes on DOTO 2 and all characters have unique abilities and
skillsets that are tailored to certain roles.


LAST-HITTING

Last-hitting involves delivering the killing blow to enemy creeps as doing so will grant you XP and gold.
There are three types of creeps – melee creeps ranged/caster creeps and siege creeps.
Understanding each type of creep is essential to securing a lot of last-hits.


THE MINI-MAP

Mini-map gives you an insight into where enemy heroes are and allows you to predict their subsequent movements.
Learning how to use the mini-map effectively will help you raise your skill level faster than you can even imagine.
The information you’re able to get from the mini-map will impact on your in-game decision making,
so you’d do well to focus on using the mini-map to its full potential.



5 BASIC MECHANICS OF DOTO 2 THAT YOU NEED TO LEARN



BASIC POSITIONING

In order to perform better in your games, you need to learn the basic positioning of your hero.
You need to look at the mini-map more often so you keep aware of the enemy and farm in the safe zone.
Similarly, you need to understand the high-ground advantage as you will have a vision
about what is in front of you and you won’t miss an attack.


KNOW WHEN THEY SEE YOU

You need to be aware of your enemy all the time.
If you are not ready to get into a team fight yet, you must know the basics of retreating.
You should be aware of the fact that the enemy team has seen you on the map and
escaping is the best option at the moment.


PRIORITIZING TOWERS AND ITEMS

Object-based gaming is very essential in Dota 2.
Taking towers when you win a team fight should be your first priority.
Similarly, understanding the item build on each hero is compulsory.
Each game brings up a new scenario and under each scenario, different item build works.


CREEPS AND TOWER AGGRO

In a lane or in a team fight, you might have to jump under the enemy tower.
In order to avoid the tower from hitting you, learning tower aggro is a very important mechanic.
Tower aggro could be used in different ways. It can be used for your benefit or for saving yourself.
Creeps can also be used to shift the tower to hit them instead of you.


COURIER MECHANICS AND HOT-KEYS

Understanding courier mechanics is very important as it may change your hero farming speed.
Your courier automatically turns into a flying courier as your hero reaches level 5 in the game.
There are options available for the courier boost and invulnerability.
